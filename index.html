Event Details

CryptoClash CTF is an exciting cybersecurity challenge designed to test participants in areas like OSINT, web exploitation, forensics, and binary exploitation. It provides a platform for ethical hackers to showcase their problem-solving skills in real-world scenarios.
Organized by the SCOPE, this event pushes competitors to think critically, analyze vulnerabilities, and apply hacking techniques strategically. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, it's a chance to prove your skills and compete for exciting rewards.
